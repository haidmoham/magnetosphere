how to use
  1. Open on a laptop or desktop — mobile browsers don't support tab audio capture
  2. Choose an audio source:
    • tab audio — in Chrome/Edge, share a tab that's playing music; the visualizer reacts to it without a mic
    • microphone — point your mic at speakers or play something out loud nearby
    • audio file — upload an mp3 or wav as a fallback
  3. Watch the particle field breathe and pulse with the music — bass drives the radial swell, mids shift the shape, treble adds sparkle
